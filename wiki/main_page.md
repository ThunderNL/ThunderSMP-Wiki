# ThunderSMP S9.2 Wiki


Welcome to the wiki of the ThunderSMP, a small Minecraft Java and Bedrock server, designed to be fun for all!

This wiki is made to compliment the [Discord server](https://www.tsmp.nl.eu.org/discord), most announcements will be made there, there is some info on there and you're required to be whitelisted there in order to join.
